RestAssured Principles

RestAssured works on 3 main principles - given | when | then

given

all input details we are giving for the API are wrapped under given method

[queryParam() , header() , body() will be in given()]

when

The operations we want to perform will be provided in when

[resource and http method will be in when]

then

validate the response[any validations will be in then() method]
